\subsection{Detailed Description}
Accesses the computer\textquotesingle{}s webcam and display the video feed in both R\+G\+B and H\+S\+V. Allows the H\+S\+V images to be filtered via sliders. After a reasonably sized object has been detected the centroid of it will be detected and marked on both videos.

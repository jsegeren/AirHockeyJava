\subsection{Detailed Description}
Encapsulates a \href{http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Row-major_order#Column-major_order}{\tt column major} 4 by 4 matrix. Like the \hyperlink{classairhockeyjava_1_1util_1_1_vector3}{Vector3} class it allows the chaining of methods by returning a reference to itself. For example\+:


\begin{DoxyPre}
\hyperlink{classairhockeyjava_1_1util_1_1_matrix4}{Matrix4} mat = new \hyperlink{classairhockeyjava_1_1util_1_1_matrix4_a4f85872fcbb4cdb2813cb639e3df5701}{Matrix4()}.trn(position).mul(camera.combined);
\end{DoxyPre}
